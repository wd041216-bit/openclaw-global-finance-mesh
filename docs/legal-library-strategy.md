# Legal Library Strategy

## Goal

Turn the product from a finance rule engine into a finance and legal operating surface that can:

- collect source material
- normalize it into searchable records
- ground model responses with citations
- feed high-value excerpts into decision and review workflows

## Stage 1

- manual ingestion
- URL ingestion
- local file ingestion
- metadata storage
- lexical search
- citation injection into chat

## Stage 2

- source quality scoring
- version history
- official-source and internal-policy separation
- pack linkage from documents to rules
- review queue for new material

## Stage 3

- jurisdiction packs generated from reviewed library content
- change detection against official sources
- replay-on-change pipeline
- richer retrieval and citation confidence controls

## Non-goal

Do not imply copyright clearance, legal sufficiency, or official completeness merely because a document exists in the library.
